Digital Collages

Hello! My name is Talia, and I am a graphic design student at the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth. I have lived in Massachusetts for all 20 years of my life. I grew up in Dover and I graduated from Dover-Sherborn High School in 2019.

Growing up, I always loved to be creative. I never considered graphic design as a career choice until my junior year of high school. In a digital photography class, I was taking, one assignment made me fall in love with digital collage. The assignment was to choose an artist and then create an art piece inspired by their work. The only requirement was that a digital photograph we had taken had to be included in the creation. After doing a bunch of research, I came across a young digital collage artist named Sean Eidder. His work festinated me. I wanted to create a digital collage as beautiful as his. I had known nothing about Adobe Photoshop, so I taught myself how to use it, and began creating. Digital collage led me to the world of graphic design, which brings me to where I am today. Although I am studying graphic design in school, I hope to be a creative director for a fashion firm and eventually have my own business where I sell my digital collage prints.
